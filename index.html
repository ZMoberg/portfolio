Who I am

I am a developer living in Los Angeles and like to simplify difficult tasks. Problem solving excites me and I enjoy bringing virtual ideas into reality. Helping others chase their dreams while gaining knowledge to reach my own goals, is what drives me.

It's important when building scalable, responsive sites and applications to use simple and readable logic. Transparency is critical in communication and production, and is a major factor in a successful product. I focus on simplicity, because often times you don't need a nuke to fix a misplaced semicolon.

YelpCamp

A campground review site created to plan, inspire and share your latest trip. By implementing user authentication it creates a personal and secure experience. It allows the user to post a full campground review with photo sharing as well as leave reviews on previously shared campgrounds. With the addition of an interactive map the user is able to save and share specific coordinates of their favorite camp spots or plan their next getaway.
